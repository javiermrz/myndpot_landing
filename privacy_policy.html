MyndPot Landing Page Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it voluntarily (e.g., through a contact form). However, we may collect certain non-personal information automatically through the use of Google Analytics:

Automatically Collected Information

  • Your IP address (anonymized, if required by applicable laws).
  • Device type and browser.
  • Pages visited and actions taken (e.g., button clicks).
  • Referring website or source (e.g., search engines, links).

This data is collected for analytical purposes only and helps us improve our website.

2. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to track your interactions on our site. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device that helps us understand how visitors interact with our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytics Cookies: These help us measure and improve the performance of our website. For example, Google Analytics uses cookies to analyze website traffic.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Understand how users interact with our website.

  • Track the effectiveness of campaigns (e.g., Google Play Store clicks).

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

Google Analytics:

To learn more about how Google collects and processes your data, visit Google’s Privacy Policy and Google Analytics Terms of Service.

5. How to Manage or Disable Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

Instructions for Common Browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Clear, enable, and manage cookies.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Manage cookies.
  • Safari: Manage cookies in Safari.
